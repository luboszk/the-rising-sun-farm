Dexter Cattle

Small but Mighty Heritage Breed

About Dexter Cattle

Dexter cattle are one of the smallest cattle breeds, originating from Ireland. They are known for their versatility and efficiency, making them perfect for small farms and sustainable agriculture.

Despite their small size, Dexters are incredibly productive and are considered a dual-purpose breed, excelling in both beef and milk production.

Key Characteristics

  • Small size: Bulls typically weigh 1,000-1,200 lbs, cows 600-800 lbs
  • Dual-purpose: Excellent for both beef and milk production
  • Hardy and adaptable to various climates
  • Known for their gentle temperament
  • Efficient grazers, perfect for small farms

Benefits

  • High-quality, well-marbled beef
  • Rich, creamy milk with high butterfat content
  • Low maintenance requirements
  • Excellent maternal instincts
  • Long productive life

Our Dexter Herd

At The Rising Sun Farm, we maintain a small but carefully selected herd of Dexter cattle. Our breeding program focuses on maintaining the breed's excellent characteristics while ensuring genetic diversity.

Our Dexters are raised on pasture, with access to natural forage and clean water. We practice rotational grazing to maintain healthy pastures and ensure our cattle have access to fresh, nutritious grass throughout the growing season.
