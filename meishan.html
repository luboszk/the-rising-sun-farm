Meishan Pigs

Ancient Chinese Heritage Breed

About Meishan Pigs

The Meishan pig is an ancient Chinese breed known for its exceptional mothering abilities and high-quality pork. They are one of the most prolific pig breeds in the world, with a history dating back thousands of years.

These pigs are characterized by their distinctive wrinkled faces and docile temperament, making them excellent for small-scale farming operations.

Key Characteristics

  • Early sexual maturity
  • Large litters (15-20 piglets)
  • Excellent mothering abilities
  • Distinctive wrinkled face
  • Docile temperament

Benefits

  • Superior pork quality
  • Excellent foraging abilities
  • Hardy and disease-resistant
  • Good feed conversion
  • Long productive life

Our Meishan Herd

At The Rising Sun Farm, we maintain a carefully selected herd of Meishan pigs. Our breeding program focuses on maintaining the breed's excellent characteristics while ensuring genetic diversity.

Our pigs are raised on pasture, with access to natural forage and clean water. We practice rotational grazing to maintain healthy pastures and ensure our pigs have access to fresh, nutritious food throughout the growing season.

Products

Pork

Our Meishan pork is known for its exceptional marbling and flavor. The meat is tender and well-suited for both traditional and modern cooking methods.

Breeding Stock

We occasionally offer breeding stock to other farmers interested in starting or expanding their Meishan herd.
