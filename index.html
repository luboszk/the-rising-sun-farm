About Our Farm

Welcome to The Rising Sun Farm, where we practice sustainable agriculture and raise heritage breeds of livestock. Our commitment to traditional farming methods and animal welfare ensures the highest quality products while preserving these valuable genetic resources for future generations.

Our Livestock

Dexter Cattle

Our Dexter cattle are a small, hardy breed known for their excellent beef and milk production. They're perfect for small farms and are known for their gentle temperament.

St. Croix Sheep

The St. Croix sheep are a hair sheep breed known for their parasite resistance and excellent meat quality. They're low-maintenance and perfect for sustainable farming.

Heritage Chickens

Our free-range chickens provide fresh eggs daily. We focus on heritage breeds that are well-suited to pasture-based systems.

Meishan Pigs

The Meishan pig is a Chinese breed known for its exceptional mothering abilities and high-quality pork. They're well-suited to pasture-based systems.

Kune Kune Pigs

Kune Kune pigs are a small, friendly breed from New Zealand. They're excellent grazers and known for their docile nature and delicious pork.
