About Our Heritage Chickens

Our heritage chicken breeds are carefully selected for their hardiness, egg production, and ability to thrive in pasture-based systems. We focus on traditional breeds that have been developed over generations for their excellent qualities.

Our chickens enjoy a free-range lifestyle, with access to fresh pasture, natural forage, and plenty of space to roam and express their natural behaviors.

Breeds We Raise

  • Rhode Island Red: Excellent egg layers with rich brown eggs
  • Barred Rock: Hardy and productive dual-purpose birds
  • Buff Orpington: Gentle giants known for their docile nature
  • Ameraucana: Colorful layers of blue and green eggs
  • Wyandotte: Beautiful birds with excellent winter laying

Benefits

  • Free-range lifestyle promotes natural behaviors
  • Natural pest control in pastures
  • Rich, flavorful eggs with deep orange yolks
  • Dual-purpose capabilities for meat and eggs
  • Strong immune systems from natural living

Our Flock Management

At The Rising Sun Farm, we practice rotational grazing with our chickens, moving them to fresh pasture regularly. This ensures they have access to fresh forage and helps maintain healthy pastures.

Our chickens are protected by mobile coops that provide shelter at night while allowing them to range freely during the day. This system helps protect them from predators while maintaining their free-range lifestyle.
